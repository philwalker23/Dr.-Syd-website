Congratulations Dr. Syd!

Incoming Dermatology Resident at Wayne State University

Hi, I’m Syd — a new dermatology resident at Wayne State University and proud Detroit native! My journey through medicine has been shaped by my commitment to community, advocacy, and mentorship. I’m especially passionate about caring for people with hidradenitis suppurativa and raising awareness for skin conditions that disproportionately affect people of color.

I’m thrilled to be training at Wayne State — not only because it’s a phenomenal dermatology program, but because it gives me the chance to care for the same communities I grew up in. There’s something incredibly powerful about coming full circle.

I’m also excited to connect with other current and future women in dermatology, and to build a medical community that uplifts and inspires. Whether you’re here as a fellow physician, student, patient, or brand — thank you for visiting! Let’s stay connected.
